Hi there! I'm Sara

I'm a  

I’m a product designer at RubiconMD. Previously, I worked as a sole designer for Martti by UpHealth (formerly Cloudbreak). I love deep diving into people’s thought processes to come up with simple solutions while driving business impact.

Prior to designing, I led customer success and experience teams for early-stage startups. During this time, I realized how much of a roadblock an inefficient and poorly designed product could be to a user. I found myself working closely with dev and design in collecting/providing feedback from our users, testing our products during dogfooding sessions, and fine-tuning our user flows. Through this experience, I became passionate about product design.

I’m based in Los Angeles but hail from Tokyo, Japan. Outside of work I enjoy traveling, going on long walks with my dogs - Miso and Soba, discovering different types of cuisine, or spending as much time at the beach or in a pool.

Causes I deeply care about:

  • Healthcare equity
  • Women’s empowerment
  • Animal welfare
